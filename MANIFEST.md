## Key Features

### 1. Complete Templates

Every template is fully written with:
- Clear instructions
- Thoughtful prompts
- Examples where helpful
- Guidance on how to use the output

You never have to wonder "what do I write here?"

### 2. Frameworks Properly Credited

All frameworks credit their originators:
- **Annual Review:** Dr. Anthony Gustin
- **Vivid Vision:** Tony Robbins / Cameron Herold
- **Ideal Life Costing:** Tim Ferriss
- **Life Map:** Alex Lieberman

Each framework is adapted and explained in CEO-friendly language.

### 3. Executive Tone

- No hustle culture
- No therapy speak
- No corporate jargon
- No productivity porn

Just calm, clear, insightful prompts designed for someone who's already accomplished and doesn't need motivation â€” just clarity.

### 4. Editable and Adaptable

Everything uses placeholders like:
- `[YOUR COMPANY]`
- `[YOUR ROLE]`
- `[YOUR STAGE OF LIFE]`
- `[YOUR CURRENT PRIORITIES]`

Personalize once, or just edit as you go.

### 5. Memory System

`memory.md` is your accumulated self-knowledge:
- Recurring patterns
- Repeated goals
- Strengths and blind spots
- Key insights from past reviews
- Themes from uploaded documents

This is what makes the system compound over time.

### 6. Upload and Pattern Detection

Drop past annual reviews, performance reviews, or notes into `uploads/`, extract patterns, and add them to `memory.md`. Your past becomes data that informs your future.
